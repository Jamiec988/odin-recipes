Chorizo Rice

Home

A quick and easy recipe that doesn't require a lot of ingredients

Ingredients
    1 Chorizo
    1 White onion
    1 Mug rice
    1 Chicken stock cube
    1 Egg
    1 Mug frozen peas
Method
    Dice the onion and chorizo into chunks
    Fry the onion on a medium to high heat for 2 minutes
    Add the chorizo and fry until it starts to brown around the edges
    Add 1 mug of rice to the pan and mix in
    Turn the heat to the lowest and add 2 mugs of boiling water
    Crumble in 1 chicken stock cube and stir, put the lid on and leave for 15 minutes
    Add 1 mug of frozen peas and leave covered for another 5 minutes
    Take 1 egg and whisk with salt and pepper
    Take the pan off the heat and quickly mix in the egg with the rice
