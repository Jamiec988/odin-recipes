Chorizo Rice

Home

A quick and easy recipe that doesn't require a lot of ingredients

Ingredients
  • 1 Chorizo
  • 1 White onion
  • 1 Mug rice
  • 1 Chicken stock cube
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Mug frozen peas
Method
  1. Dice the onion and chorizo into chunks
  2. Fry the onion on a medium to high heat for 2 minutes
  3. Add the chorizo and fry until it starts to brown around the edges
  4. Add 1 mug of rice to the pan and mix in
  5. Turn the heat to the lowest and add 2 mugs of boiling water
  6. Crumble in 1 chicken stock cube and stir, put the lid on and leave for 15 minutes
  7. Add 1 mug of frozen peas and leave covered for another 5 minutes
  8. Take 1 egg and whisk with salt and pepper
  9. Take the pan off the heat and quickly mix in the egg with the rice
